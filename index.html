About

Hi, I'm Alan Dai.
I am collaborative software engineer and an avid boulderer based in the San Francisco, CA.

Back in Dec 2019, I graduated from UC Berkeley with a major in political economy and a minor in computer science. I originally planned to pursue business analytics but being surrounded by engineers got me interested in software development. Graduating into the COVID job market gave me the time I needed to commit to software engineering. Mid-January this year, after 16 challenging weeks and 3 major projects, I completed the full-stack immersive program at App Academy. I loved every minute of it, from working with others to building out meaningful web apps. Now, I feel confident in my own ability to build software from start to finish but there's a lot to learn. I am looking forward to tackling new challenges and mastering new technologies.

In my free time, you can find me spending way too much time in local climbing gyms or looking for the best sourdough in the Bay Area. If you've got a bakery to recommend or want to climb with me, hit me up - I'm always down for an adventure!

Projects

This is a selection of my recent full-stack projects.

SlapCloud

SlapCloud is a clone of SoundCloud, a music hosting and sharing platform that I use everyday. I was drawn to how functional and responsive SoundCloud's UI was and wanted to recreate it.
Built using React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, and PostgreSQL.

Dreamers

I got the opportunity to work with a focused, talented team to build Dreamers, a social media app to share dreams and aspirations. We wanted to provide a space where positivity can be spread in this increasingly remote and digital time.
Built using the MongoDB, Express.js, React/Redux, and Node.js.

Milk

The pandemic has reshaped the way we interact with others. I wanted to capture this challenge in a simple but interactive game, where you, as the player, must maintain your distance in a hectic world.
Built using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
